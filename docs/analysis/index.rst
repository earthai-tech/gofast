*********
Analysis
*********

.. currentmodule:: gofast.analysis

The :mod:`gofast.analysis` module provides a comprehensive suite of tools for performing various types of analyses, including factor analysis, dimensionality reduction, and decompositions. It is composed of several submodules designed to cater to different analytical needs, such as identifying latent variables, evaluating noise impact on models, and applying rotations for better interpretability of factor loadings.
